Jewel Of Nizam - The Minar

This award-winning authentic Hyderabadi cuisine restaurant is also called Hyderabad's fifth Minar. Take in the spectacular views of the Osman Sagar as you are perched 100 feet above, relishing the aromas and flavours of Nizami cuisine, recipes of which are priceless gems from a royal repast.
